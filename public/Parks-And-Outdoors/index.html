Parks and outdoor spaces in London

You are here: Home > Things to do > Parks and outdoors

Thanks to its abundance of green spaces and outdoor activities, London is the world's first National Park City. Make the most of the summer months with London's outdoor spaces, where you can enjoy picturesque park strolls, grab a bite to eat at an alfresco restaurant, bag a bargain at outdoor markets in London or make a summer splash at an outdoor pool. Relax in the best London parks and visit world-famous spots such as Kew Gardens and Hyde Park.
