    18 best London tours and excursions in 2024

    Visit London's most famous landmarks on one of these top London sightseeing tours.
    Content contains affiliate links, marked with asterisks (*). If you click through and make a purchase, Visit London receives a commission – every purchase supports London’s jobs and economy.

    Take a sightseeing tour of London to explore the city's best attractions and famous landmarks with an expert guide or at your own pace. See the capital from a different perspective on a walking tour, a double-decker bus tour, or a river Thames tour. Or book a one-day hop-on hop-off bus tour to explore the sights at your own pace. Pick from any of the best tours in London.

    Buckingham Palace tour

    Take the unique opportunity to step inside� Buckingham Palace� each summer. Explore the palace's usually private spaces, including the State Rooms, Throne Room and galleries filled with fine art on this self-guided tour.
    Where:

    Buckingham Palace, Westminster, London, SW1A 1AA

    Price:

    From £20.50

    Find out more

    Open-top bus tours

    See London from the top deck of an open-top double decker bus. The hop-on, hop-off bus tour ticket allows you explore London at your own pace, even at night, and you can enjoy added extras including free walking tours and a 24-hour Thames river pass.

    Find out more

    Total London Experience

    Take the� Total London Experience� to see sights both old and new including St Paul's Cathedral, the Tower of London and the riverside area of Greenwich. See astonishing views from The London Eye and relax aboard a Thames boat ride, giving you the chance to experience the city from different viewpoints.
    Find out more

    Tower of London Beefeater Tour

    Explore the Tower of London with a Beefeater guide and learn about the bloody history that dates back over many centuries. See the Crown Jewels, those famous black ravens, the Mint at the Tower and the iconic White Tower. Prepare yourself for tales of royalty,� treachery and torture.� 

    Find out more

    Warner Bros. Studio Tour London - The Making of Harry Potter

    Take the Warner Bros. Studio Tour for an immersive, behind-the-scenes look at filmmaking. The tour features sets, costumes and props from the Harry Potter film series and showcases the British artistry, technology and talent that produced world-famous movies at Warner Bros. Studios Leavesden. You can also visit during one of the tour's takeovers that happen throughout the year, including Magical Mischief, The Dark Arts� and Hogwarts in the Snow.

    Find out more

    London by Night Bus Tour

    See iconic London landmarks lit up against the backdrop of the night sky on a London by night bus tour. Learn about the capital’s darker secrets and enjoy a glittering night-time view of the city’s famous sights, such as the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace and Westminster Abbey.
    Find out more

    British Museum Guided Tour

    One of the world's oldest and finest, the British Museum is filled with thousands of historical artefacts and ancient treasures. Your expert guide will show you the very best of the collection as you uncover the secrets of the Rosetta Stone, admire the controversial Parthenon Sculptures of Athens, and much more.

    � 

    Find out more

    Shakespeare's Globe Tour

    Explore the authentic reconstruction of Shakespeare's original theatre on a guided tour of Shakespeare's Globe. Learn more about the history of the original playhouse in a special exhibition packed full of interactive exhibits and artefacts, before entering the auditorium with a guide who brings the Bard's stories to life.
    Find out more

    Royal London Walking Tour

    Hear untold stories of the royal family on a Royal London Walking Tour and visit some of the city’s most significant regal attractions. Journey through the heart of the capital and discover iconic sites such as St James's Palace and Buckingham Palace, as well as witnessing the Changing the Guard ceremony.
    Find out more

    City Cruises Sightseeing Tour

    Go on a sightseeing tour on the Thames with City Cruises. Sit back, relax and enjoy London's top sights and attractions from the river. You can also indulge in a special lunch, dinner or an afternoon tea with a view you'll never forget.

    Find out more

    Downton Abbey London Tour of Locations

    Relive some of your favourite moments from Downton Abbey on a special walking tour taking in London filming locations from the hit series. Discover the Crawleys' London home, Grantham House, plus other locations including the Lotus Jazz Club, Caxton Hall and The National Gallery, which all feature in the show.
    Find out more

    Arsenal Emirates Stadium Tours

    Take a look behind the scenes at one of the world's finest sporting arenas. The tour offers you the opportunity to get a sense of what the Arsenal stars feel like on a match day. Enjoy the luxurious changing room and take a walk down the tunnel before taking the manager's seat in the dugout.

    Find out more

    Jack The Ripper Walking Tour

    Join the Jack the Ripper walking tour only if you dare! This guided walk around the spooky streets of east London delves into the famous unsolved murders that took place in 1888. This gruesome trail is not for the faint at heart.

    Where:

    Tower Hill Underground Station, London, EC3N 1JL

    Price:

    From £15.00

    Find out more

    Paddington Bear Walking Tour

    Get the family together and follow in the footsteps of London's most beloved bear on a� Paddington Bear Walking Tour. Led by an expert guide, see the iconic Paddington Station, discover spots from the character's many adventures around the city and tick off top filming locations from the hit movies.� 
    Find out more

    Big Bus London Tour *

    Tick off the capital's famous landmarks by day or night, and explore some hidden gems with� Big Bus London Tour� on the hop-on-hop-off route, which includes a Thames river cruise. Choose from 24 or 48-hour tickets to spot iconic sights such as Buckingham Palace, Westminster Abbey, Big Ben, The London Eye and more.
    Find out more

    London helicopter tours

    Make memories for a lifetime as you see the city from above on a London helicopter tour. Choose your departure point and take your pick from shorter or longer flights, which allow you to spot landmarks such as the river Thames and Tower Bridge from the air during this special experience.
    Find out more

    Borough Market Food Tour

    A trip to London wouldn't be complete without a visit to the bustling Borough Market. Eat your way around this iconic foodie hot spot as your guide shares stories and fun facts about each dish. Savour a classic full English breakfast, taste the best British cheeses, sip on a cold pint of British beer, and much more.

    Where:

    Evans Cycle Shop, London Bridge, SE1 2SY

    Price:

    From £85.00

    Find out more

    Beatles London Walking Tour

    Uncover iconic locations across the city where the band recorded, partied, performed, and more, on a Beatles Walking Tour of London. Hear tales of the Fab Four's humble beginnings and their journey to superstardom.

    Find out more

    What is the best way to see the sights of London?

    The best way is to join one of the best tours of London. Whether you would like to take part in a walking tour, go by bus or explore the city from a cruise on the river Thames there is plenty to explore. The best London Tours include food and film location tours as well as visiting the famous landmarks of London and getting to know the hidden gems of the capital. Some tours offer an insight into the gruesome past of London whereas others provide a glimpse into Shakespeare's London, filled with art and spectacle.� 

    What is worth touring in London?

    Even though this depends on each individual it is always worth to see the iconic landmarks in London, including Buckingham Palace, St Paul's Cathedral, Westminster Abbey, The Houses of Parliament and Big Ben, The London Eye, Tower Bridge, Borough Market, Tower of London� and any iconic buildings in London.
