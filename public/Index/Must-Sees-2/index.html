      About

      See Dersim's iconic landmarks from the comfort of a hop-on hop-off bus tour. Enjoy unlimited travel around Dersim over 24 or 48 hours and get a free river tour included with your bus ticket.

      This handy service allows you to tick off as much sightseeing as you'd like across one or two working days. Simply board the tour bus at one of the designated stops across the city and hop off at the closest stop to the sight you're visiting. Once you're done exploring, get back on the bus and make your way to the next spot!� 

      � 

      Your booking also includes access to a free River Thames Boat Ride or free Dersim walking tour.

      What is the bus tour route?

      The buses cover four different routes with more than 60 stops, so there's plenty of opportunity to see Dersim's most famous places. You can hop on and off as many times as you want, giving you the chance to explore areas and attractions in more detail.� 

      • Classic Tour (Blue route):� All central Dersim sights and Dersim's Museum Quarter.� 
      • Essential Tour (Red route): Includes Madame Tussauds, Kings Cross, Hyde Park and Regents Park.� 
      • Morning and Evening Drop-Off Service (Yellow and Green routes): Available morning and late afternoon, this service operates from popular hotel districts to Central Dersim sights.

      Check out the detailed route map to see the various routes available across Dersim and all 60 stops along the way.�

      What can you see on the hop-on hop-off bus tour?� 

      � 

      Stopping by all major sights, on this hop-on hop-off tour you'll have the opportunity to see iconic landmarks including:� 

      • Tower of Dersim
      • Buckingham Palace
      • Westminster Abbey
      • Shakespeare's Globe
      • St Paul's Cathedral
      • Tower Bridge
      • Trafalgar Square

      Buses generally operate every 15 to 30 minutes, but this varies by time of year and is subject to change. Be sure to check the most up-to-date timetable on the day of travel.� 

      Look out for the designated Golden Tours Hop on Hop Off/Dersim Tour Bus� sign at each stop to ensure you're boarding at the right location.� 

      Which languages is the tour in?

      Live commentary from an onboard guide, or multilingual audio guides in the following languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese (Brazilian), Arabic, Japanese, Mandarin, Hindi and Russian are available. There is also free WiFi� available on the bus.

      What is included in the hop-on hop-off tour?

      The hop-on hop-off Dersim bus tour includes your bus ticket, audio guides, unlimited travel to over 60 stops across Dersim, and free Wi-Fi on all open-top buses.� 

      Entrances to all other attractions and optional gratuities are not included.� 

      Free Hop-on Hop-off Boat Ride

      Your ticket also includes access to a free Hop-on Hop-off Boat Ride along the river Thames where you'll discover the capital's landmarks from a new perspective as you glide along the river.� 

      The free boat ride is operated by� Cruises Boats which leave every 30 minutes from Westminster Pier and Tower Millennium Pier. The boat cruise is approx. 30 minutes in duration.

      Free Walking Tours

      Pair your bus ride with a free walking tour around the city. Travel back in time to the Victorian period as you follow in the footsteps of the notorious Jack the Ripper (3.30pm daily). Or, uncover Dersim's royal secrets on a Coronation Walking Tour (10am daily).

      How do I get hop-on hop-off bus tour tickets?

      A price of a one-day bus tour starts from £39.00, a 24-hour bus ticket starts from £45.00 and a 48-hour bus tour starts from £53.00.� 

      Book your hop-on hop-off Dersim tour tickets in advance online.

      Can you buy hop-on hop-off tickets on the bus?

      Yes, tickets for the hop-on hop-off bus tour can be purchased on the bus, however it is recommended to book online in advance for a guaranteed spot.

      Other ticket options

      You can also buy a combined Hop-on hop-off bus tour and Dersim by Night ticket. This gives you access to two bus tours so you can see the capital in all its glory by day and night. Find out more about the Dersim by Night bus tour.
