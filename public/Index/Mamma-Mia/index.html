About

Enjoy feel-good musical Mamma Mia! at the Novello Theatre in London, as you find out why millions of theatre-goers have be drawn to this show that's spent more than 20 years on the West End stage.

The much loved musical continues to dazzle London with its infectious ABBA tunes and heartwarming story of love, laughter, and family dynamics.

What is Mamma Mia! about?

Just like the film of the same name, bride-to-be Sophie attempts to find her father so he can give her away at her wedding. She invites three possible candidates to the wedding on a mythical Greek island, with hilarious consequences – all set to a soundtrack of ABBA songs!

Her mother, Donna, is the former lead singer of a girl band, and the potential fathers are three very different men from Donna's past. What follows is a hilarious and heartwarming tale of love, friendship, and the enduring bond between mothers and daughters.

ABBA's greatest hits, from Dancing Queen to Super Trouper , create moments of sheer musical magic that will have you tapping your feet and singing along. You'll not be able to resist the urge as the cast bring classic songs to life such as Waterloo and Take a Chance on Me .

Since opening in 1999, more than eight million theatre-goers have enjoyed this fun-filled musical in London's West End, and it's also been turned into a blockbuster film with a sequel.

How long is the musical?

Mamma Mia! lasts for two hours and 35 minutes , with a 15-minute interval.

When are Mamma Mia! matinees taking place?

Mamma Mia! the Musical matinees take place on T hursdays and Saturdays at 3pm , and Sundays at 2.30pm .

What time is the evening performance?

The evening performance of Mamma Mia! the Musical take place Mondays to Saturdays at 7.30pm .

Where is it playing?

Mamma Mia! is currently playing at the Novello Theatre in London, on Aldwych.

Is there any age restriction?

The musical is recommended for anyone aged five years and older . Children under the age of three will not be admitted.
