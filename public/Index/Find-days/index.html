What countries can you visit in a day from London?

With some of the best transport links in the world there are many fun destinations you can visit in a day from London.

Hopping aboard the Eurostar � from St Pancras International, you can be whisked away to four exciting countries including France, Belgium, The Netherlands and Germany. Explore the cobbled streets of Amsterdam, sample beer, waffles and chocolate in Brussels and uncover the history and architecture of Cologne.�

Paris, the 'City of Love', is one of the most exciting spots to visit on a day trip from London. Discover the city at your leisure with a river cruise, bus tour or travel car. Or opt for an escorted day trip and visit the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum and a scenic trip along the Seine.
