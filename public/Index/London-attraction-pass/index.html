      How does it work?

      1. Go to The London Pass booking page .
      2. Choose a duration that suits your trip – The London Pass is available for one, two, three, four, five, six, seven or 10 consecutive days.
      3. Download the pass to your phone.
      4. Use the London Pass app to plan your sightseeing adventure.
      5. Show your London Pass at the gate of any participating attraction and head straight inside.

      You have two years to activate your London Pass from the date of purchase.
