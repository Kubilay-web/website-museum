What can you experience in London?

As one of the most unique cities in the world, London has an abundance of fun and exciting experiences just waiting to be seen! From world-famous museums and galleries to historical palaces and unique activities, discover the best experiences right here in London.

Tourists and residents are guaranteed to find unique, fun and original things to do in the city. From immersive film set tours and relaxing cruises on the Thames, to indulgent themed afternoon teas and interactive art exhibitions, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Uncover quirky themed immersive experiences taking over London. From theatrical bar experiences to activity bars, it’s time to embrace the fun and unusual side of the capital.

What are some of the best experiences in London?

Discover a variety of experiences on offer in London, from art shows and historical palace tours to escape rooms and secret speakeasy bars. Here are some handpicked experiences in London worth trying:

  • Enjoy a self-guided tour of the iconic Buckingham Palace , open for a limited time each summer.
  • See original sets, props and costumes from the Harry Potter film series at Warner Bros. Studio Tour London .
  • Step into the ultimate immersive art experience and see art from the world’s greatest painters come to life at Frameless .
  • Play your part as a detective and solve puzzles in the immersive escape game Sherlock: The Official Live Game .
  • Visit the popular prison-themed cocktail experience Alcotraz , don your orange jumpsuit and smuggle liquor past the wardens to create bespoke cocktails.
