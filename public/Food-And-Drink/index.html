What food and drink is London most famous for?

When it comes to the London food and drink scene many popular traditional dishes will probably come to mind. From fish and chips to a roast dinner, full English breakfast to pie and mash, London has an incredible amount of deliciously mouthwatering options to try. Don’t forget to try a classic dessert in London; most notably the sticky toffee pudding served with custard or ice cream.

Make sure to save time to satisfy another classic London foodie tradition: afternoon tea. Enjoy a variety of savoury and sweet treats, served with a selection of teas, and you can even upgrade your experience with champagne.
