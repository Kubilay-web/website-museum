About

Laugh along to musical comedy The Book of Mormon at the Prince of Wales Theatre near Leicester Square.

Winner of multiple awards, including four Oliviers and nine Tony Awards, this Broadway musical from the creators of South Park, Trey Parker and Matt Stone , and Avenue Q co-creator Robert Lopez, has been a West End musical hit with UK audiences since 2013.

What is The Book of Mormon musical about?

The Book of Mormon is a satirical musical telling the story of two young Mormon missionaries who travel to Uganda in Africa to preach the Mormon religion, only to discover they have a lot more to learn about the real world than they have to teach about Mormonism.

Follow the journey of Elder Price and Elder Cunningham as they naively try to convert the locals, who are more worried about war, famine, poverty and AIDS than religion. Elder Price is a rising star in his church and a shining example for Mormons around him, whereas Elder Cunningham’s awkward personality and inability to see “God’s plan” makes their journey a little more chaotic.

Whilst the missionaries are concerned that they can’t convert their new neighbours to Mormonism, the locals are doing all that they can to survive in a war-torn country. When it’s all becoming too much and they are almost defeated, they stumble upon a local young woman named Nabulungi, who believes everything that Elder Cunningham preaches and dreams of a simpler life – so can they convert the locals to Mormonism after all?

This West End classic will leave you in stitches of laughter, so book tickets today to see The Book of Mormon in London.

The Book of Mormon London cast

The current London cast of The Book of Mormon musical includes:

  • Elder Price - Dom Simpson
  • Elder Cunningham - Conner Peirson
  • Nabulungi - Leanne Robinson
  • Elder McKinley/Moroni - Steven Webb
  • Mafala Hatimbi - Richard Lloyd-King
  • Joseph Smith/Jesus - Gavin Alex
  • General/Satan - Edward Baruwa

Please note that the cast and line-up are subject to change.

The Book of Mormon is choreographed by Casey Nicholaw who also co-directs with Trey Parker. The musical features a diverse range of catchy and humorous songs. From the opening number “Hello!” to introduce the missionaries to the enthusiastic “Two by Two! and the clever and hilarious take on the villagers’ response with the song “Hasa Diga Eebowai”, there are lots of hilarious songs to add to the story.

Evening performances of The Book of Mormon usually take place at 7.30pm, from Mondays to Saturdays .

On which days are there matinee performances of The Book of Mormon London?

The Book of Mormon matinees are on Fridays and Saturdays , usually at 2.30pm - although times and days may vary. Make sure to check your ticket in advance.

What age is it appropriate for?

The Book of Mormon contains explicit language and adult themes. Parental guidance is advised and under-16s must be accompanied by, and sat next to, an adult.
