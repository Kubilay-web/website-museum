Animal attractions and zoos in London

London Zoo

Explore habitats and join talks at this famous zoo.

Chessington World of Adventures

See lions, penguins and more between theme park rides.

SEA LIFE London

Spot more than 500 species at this underwater world.

Whipsnade Zoo

Visit the UK’s largest zoo, a short trip from London.

See animals from all over the world at the best zoos in London and encounter a variety of animals at one of the many city farms dotted around the capital. Discover a more tranquil side of London as you explore incredible animal sanctuaries and wildlife parks in London.

Wildlife parks in London

London Wetland Centre

Get up close to otters and water birds at this tranquil nature reserve.

Battersea Park Children's Zoo

Make furry and feathery friends at this fun-filled central London zoo.

Richmond Park

Spot the deer and other wildlife across 2,500 acres in the city's largest park.

Bushy Park

Wander Henry VIII’s favourite park, where you can see wild red and fallow deer.

Belmont Children's Farm

Kentish Town City Farm
